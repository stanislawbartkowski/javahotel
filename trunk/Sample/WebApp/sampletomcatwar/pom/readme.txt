-------------------------------------------
-- TOMCAT (tested with Tomcat 7.0)
-------------------------------------------
* Prerequisites (should be available on classpath, also can be put in tomcat lib directory)
1) Eclipselink (http://www.eclipse.org/eclipselink/) : eclipselink.jar (also javax.inject.jar and javax.persistence.jar)
2) Derby (http://db.apache.org/derby/) : derby.jar

* Deploy jar
* http://{host}:{port}/webjython/
